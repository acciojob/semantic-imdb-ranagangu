Summary

The Shawshank Redemption tells the powerful story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, who is sentenced to life in Shawshank prison. While enduring the harsh realities of prison life, he befriends Red, a long-term inmate. Over the course of two decades, Andy retains hope and dignity, eventually orchestrating one of the most brilliant escapes in cinematic history.
