Summary

*Inception* is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie explores the concept of dreams within dreams and follows a team of skilled thieves who infiltrate the subconscious to steal or plant information. With stunning visuals, a powerful cast, and a thought-provoking storyline, it challenges the viewer's perception of reality.
